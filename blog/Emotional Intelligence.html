Emotional Intelligence

1. What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is having a real self awareness. Being able to understand your own emotions, to evaluate them in certain situations and to control and manage them. People with High EQ's are able to work well with others as they can understand themselves and perceive others as well.

2. How is it different to IQ?

IQ is more about academic intelligence. Someone with a high IQ may be the smartest person in the room, but if they have a low EQ they may confused between their emotions and not understand why they did or the impact it can have on other people when it happen.

3. Why is it important?

It's important as it will mean you have a better life in general. You will be happier as you can be present and mindful in a moment. But it will also mean that you are probably better choice when its come to co-op work because you understand others as well as yourself.
